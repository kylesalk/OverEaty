title: Crab Stuffed Mushrooms

time: 15 min. prep / 30 min. bake

ingredient: 12 ounces flaked crab
ingredient: 4 tbsp finely chopped onions
ingredient: 1 tsp dry mustard
ingredient: 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
ingredient: 2 tsp parsley
ingredient: 1/8 tsp ground red pepper
ingredient: 1/8 tsp black pepper
ingredient: 1 egg
ingredient: 1 tbsp mayonnaise
ingredient: 1/8 tsp garlic salt
ingredient: 1/4 cup butter
ingredient: 1/2 cup dry breadcrumbs

stage: Preparation
step: Mix all ingredients except garlic, butter, and breadcrumbs
step: Stuff the mixed ingredients into the mushrooms
step: Melt the butter
step: Add garlic to the melted butter
step: Pour the butter/garlic mix over the mushrooms
step: Sprinkle the breadcrumbs across the top of the mushrooms

stage: Bake
step: Add olive oil to mushrooms in a pan
step: 415F for 30 min.
