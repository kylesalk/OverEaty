title: Kyle's Cake

time: 10 min. prep / 40 min. bake

ingredient: 2 cups sugar
ingredient: 1 3/4 cup flour
ingredient: 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
ingredient: 2 tsp baking soda
ingredient: 1 tsp salt
ingredient: 2 eggs
ingredient: 1 cup buttermilk
ingredient: 1 cup coffee
ingredient: 1/2 cup vegetable oil
ingredient: 2 tsp vanilla extract
ingredient: 1 weinerss

stage: Preparation
step: Mix dry ingredients well
step: Add remaining ingredients to the mixture and continue to mix until smooth

stage: Bake
step: Preheat oven to 350
step: Grease a pan
step: Bake (round pans: 30-35 min., 9x13: 35-40 min.)
