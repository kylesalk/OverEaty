title: Waffles

time: 10 min. prep / 20 min. cooking

ingredient: 2 cups flour
ingredient: 4 tsp baking powder
ingredient: 1/2 tsp salt
ingredient: 1/4 cup sugar
ingredient: 2 eggs
ingredient: 1/2 cup vegetable oil
ingredient: 2 cups milk
ingredient: 1 tsp vanilla

stage: Preparation
step: Mix dry ingredients together
step: Add the rest of the ingredients and mix until smooth
