title: Pork Loin

time: 10 min. prep / 50 min. cook

ingredient: 1 tsp salt
ingredient: 1 tsp pepper
ingredient: 1 tsp italian seasoning
ingredient: 1 tsp rosemary
ingredient: 1 tsp garlic powder
ingredient: 1 tsp ground coriander
ingredient: 2 tbsp olive oil

stage: Preperation
step: Mix all dry ingredients
step: Apply mix as a rub to the pork loin
step: Sear all sides of the pork loin

stage: Cook
step: 400F for about 50 min.
