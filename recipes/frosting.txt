title: Kyle's Frosting

time: 

ingredient: 1 1/2 cups butter (3 sticks), softened
ingredient: 1 cup unsweetened cocoa
ingredient: 5 cups powdered sugar
ingredient: 2 tsp vanilla extract
ingredient: 1/2 cup milk or cream

stage: Preparation
step: Melt butter into the milk, then add vanilla
step: Add cocoa to large mixing bowl
step: Add 1 cup sugar and 1 tbsp milk into cocoa while mixing
step: Repeat previous steps until all ingredients have been mixed thoroughly
