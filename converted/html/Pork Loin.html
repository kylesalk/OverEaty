Pork Loin

10 min. prep / 50 min. cook

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Preperation

  1. Mix all dry ingredients
  2. Apply mix as a rub to the pork loin
  3. Sear all sides of the pork loin

Cook

  1. 400F for about 50 min.
